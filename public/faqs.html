Are scholarships and financial assistance available?

Yes! Please fill out the Scholarship form by downloading it from the registration section, and then you can either email or mail it to the registrar.

Can I request to share a room with friends and family?

William Sears Bahá'í School will make every effort to accommodate requests but does not make any guarantees.
Please list the names of the individuals you'd like to room with in your registration form. 

Note: 
All rooms must have at least one adult (over 21 years of age) if anyone in the room is under the age of 18 years. 
All rooms are single gender, with the exception of families and sponsoring adults.

I want to attend but I am under 18 years of age, can I still attend?

All youth, junior youth, and children must have a sponsoring adult (21 years of age and older) attending the Summer School with them. To request a sponsor please fill out and submit the form below.

I have food allergies or am a vegetarian, can I still eat in the Dining Hall?

William Sears Bahá'í School will make every effort to accommodate requests but does not make any guarantees.

All meals will provide a vegetarian and gluten free option, please state that you request vegetarian or gluten free meals in your registration. 
If you have food allergies, please clearly state them in your registration form and the Committee will contact you to better accommodate and understand needs. 

Can I place my child in the next grade?

William Sears Bahá'í School strongly encourages all children to participate in their respective grade classes. Curriculum is carefully prepared to be age appropriate and is not always suitable or appropriate for a younger audience.  Participants will automatically be placed in their current grade.
