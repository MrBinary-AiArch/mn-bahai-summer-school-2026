REGISTRATION IS OPEN!
Register!

Registration Fees & Timeline
(fees do not include lodging and/or meals)

Early Bird Pricing (ends 30 June): $55/Adult*, $20/Child

1 July to 21 July: $65/Adult, $25/Child

22 July to 30 July: $75/Adult, $30/Child

Single Day Registration Fee: $25/Adult, $10/Child

*Adult is defined as 15+.
Children/Infants ages 0-2 do not pay. 

If you need to register by mail, please contact the Registrar at wmsears-reg@usbnc.org or 651-356-8897

Families are welcome to share rooms with other families. Rooms have up to 8 beds.

Payment, in full, required at time of registration; please contact the Registrar if other arrangements are necessary:

Email:       wmsears-reg@nbs.usbnc.org
Phone:     651-356-8897
     

Scholarships & Financial Assistance

William Sears Baha'i School offers financial assistance on an as needed basis. 

If you are registering by mail and require financial assistance, please download the form and submit it with your registration form. 
Cancellation Policy

Registration fees are non-refundable.

Remaining registration costs are refundable per the timeline below.

There will be no refunds the week of the School.
Until 1 July - 75% Refundable
Until 15 July - 50% Refundable
Until 29 July - 25% Refundable
